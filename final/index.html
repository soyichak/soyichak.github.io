Final Project Pitch

For my final project, I want to build an interactive planner website where users can add study plans onto a calendar, keep a to-do list, and decorate their calendar space.

Project Title: Study Ritual Planner

Calendar ¬∑ To-Do List ¬∑ Custom Decoration

Concept

The Study Ritual Planner is a small web app that combines a monthly calendar, a to-do list, and simple decorative tools. Users can click on a day to add their study plans, track general tasks in a to-do list, and personalize their calendar with colors and emoji-like stickers. The goal is to create a cozy digital space that makes planning feel more intentional and less stressful.

Goals

  • Let users add and view study plans for specific calendar days.
  • Provide a simple to-do list for tasks that are not tied to a single day.
  • Offer basic decoration options so users can change theme colors and add stickers.

Core Features

  • Clickable calendar: A grid of days where clicking a date selects it and shows plans for that day.
  • Day-based plans: Input box to add plans for the selected day, with a list that updates dynamically using JavaScript.
  • To-do list: Separate list for general tasks, with checkboxes to mark items as done and buttons to delete them.
